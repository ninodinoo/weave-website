weave

Your AI workflow, woven to perfection.

A framework that lives inside your AI coding tool.
It learns how you work and builds the perfect workflow — automatically.

↓

How it works

01

Install

$ npx weave-ai

One command. Weave detects your AI tools, installs native skills, agents, and hooks for each platform.

02

Onboard

$ /weave:onboarding

A short conversation. Weave learns who you are, what you're building, and how you like to work. Then it generates a personalized workflow.

03

Work

$ just work.

Agents handle the heavy lifting. Teams coordinate complex tasks. Your workflow evolves in the background as your project grows.

Features

💬

Conversation-based onboarding

No forms, no config files. Weave interviews you and analyzes your codebase to build a workflow that actually fits.

🤖

Custom agents

Specialized AI personas tailored to your stack. A reviewer that knows your ORM, a tester that matches your framework.

👥

Agent teams

Groups of agents with defined workflows. Planner → Coder → Reviewer → Tester — with handoffs and rejection handling.

🔄

Auto-evolution

Hooks track how you work. When patterns change, Weave proposes targeted improvements. Nothing changes without approval.

🔗

Multi-platform sync

One workflow, every AI tool. Set it up once, sync to Claude Code, OpenClaw, Cursor, and Codex in native format.

🚪

Zero lock-in

Don't like it? npx weave-ai uninstall removes everything. Your project stays exactly as it was.
